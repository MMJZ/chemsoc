For the first time in the history of our society we are offering stash! Since we have never done this before, we need to gauge interest before ordering. You can find the link to express interest below (at this point, putting your name down doesn’t commit you to buy anything).

We are very excited about the design, which features a pastel periodic table on the front of a navy sweatshirt, with the OUCB logo on the back. The text below the periodic table reads “fig. 1. The Oxford University Chemistry and Biochemistry Society”. The printing is high quality screen printing.

The price for members is £20, and £30 for non members. We hope to take orders at the end of freshers’ week 2018, which means the sweatshirts would be ready to give out in about 2nd or 3rd week of Michaelmas.

We hope you are all as excited as we are about this venture! If you are, please express your interest by signing up!
