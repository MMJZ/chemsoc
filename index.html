About

The Oxford University Chemistry Society is one of the largest societies in the University, now boasting a membership of several hundred students studying a wide range of subjects. We are dedicated to enhancing the student experience of our members, hosting a range of interesting speakers who are experts in their respective fields. We also run extremely popular socials to give a chance for students to enjoy themselves and mix with people from throughout the university.
